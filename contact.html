Consultations & Pining Fee

Some alteration consultations for bridal gowns or special occasion dresses may require additional time due to the complexity of the garment or the number of questions about the alteration process. In such cases, a Pinning Fee will apply. This fee is non-refundable but will be credited toward the total cost of the alteration if the client proceeds with the service.

Cancellation Policy

Once a garment has been dropped off for alterations, it is added to the work schedule and may enter the cutting or fitting process. If the client decides to cancel after leaving the garment, a Cancellation Fee will be charged. The garment will be returned in its current condition at the time of cancellation, and no refunds will be issued for any work already started.

Disclaimer

Scheduling a consultation or appointment does not guarantee that we will be able to complete your project. RN Luxury Couture reserves the right to decline certain projects based on current workload, time frame, or design capacity.

Appointment Policy

Appointments are required, walk-ins are not accepted if we are not available.
