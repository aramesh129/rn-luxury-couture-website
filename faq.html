Standard turnaround time: Approximately two weeks from your first appointment, provided the alteration does not require additional fittings. This time can vary because sometimes we must finish one adjustment (like the hem) before proceeding to the next (like the bodice adjustment), which extends the overall process. We will give you a precise time estimate during your consultation.

Rush service: Yes, we accept rush orders subject to our current availability. A service is considered urgent if the turnaround time is 2 weeks or less. These orders carry a rush fee starting at $25, which may vary based on the complexity of the garment.

The final fit is approved based on your current body size. RN Luxury Cousute LLC. is not responsible for changes in fit due to subsequent weight fluctuations. Any additional alteration required due to these changes will be treated as a new service and will incur additional charges.

We understand that most of our dresses are high-value and delicate. We always handle your garment with the utmost care and respect. However, altering existing garments, especially those with delicate fabrics, lace, beading, or vintage pieces, carries an inherent risk.

Acknowledged Risks: During the process, pre-existing defects, age-related fragility, or weaknesses in the fabric may become apparent. RN Luxury Couture LLC. is not responsible for damage, tearing, or discoloration caused by these underlying conditions of the garment.

Adjustments Not Recommended: If certain extreme adjustments are not recommended due to the fabric or construction, and the client chooses to proceed under this warning, the work is done at the client's own risk.

Our Commitment: In the unlikely event of confirmed damage resulting from gross negligence by our team, our monetary liability is strictly limited to the cost of the alteration services you have paid.

The remaining balance must be paid at the time of the final garment pickup. We accept payments only in Cash or the Venmo App.

If you do not pick up your garment within 30 days after being notified of completion, a daily Storage Fee will be applied. Garments not picked up after 60 days will be considered abandoned and may be disposed of at the Service Provider's discretion.

Consultations & Pining Fee

Some alteration consultations for bridal gowns or special occasion dresses may require additional time due to the complexity of the garment or the number of questions about the alteration process. In such cases, a Pinning Fee will apply. This fee is non-refundable but will be credited toward the total cost of the alteration if the client proceeds with the service.

Cancellation Policy

Once a garment has been dropped off for alterations, it is added to the work schedule and may enter the cutting or fitting process. If the client decides to cancel after leaving the garment, a Cancellation Fee will be charged. The garment will be returned in its current condition at the time of cancellation, and no refunds will be issued for any work already started.

Disclaimer
