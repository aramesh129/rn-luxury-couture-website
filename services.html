OUR SERVICES

You've invested time and emotion into choosing the perfect dress. Now, let us guide you through the most important investment: the perfect fit. the perfect fit.

ALTERATIONS

At RN Luxury Couture LLC., we know a dress isn't truly ready until it is precisely adjusted to your body. Professional tailoring is what turns a beautiful garment into your spectacular garment, ensuring you move with grace and confidence.

Comfortable Length: We tailor the length to be comfortable and functional, adapted to your shoes and the event setting, ensuring you can walk, dance, and enjoy yourself worry-free.

Investment Value: Expert tailoring protects the investment you've made in your outfit, ensuring every detail enhances your figure.

We are your trusted modistes, specializing in high-value and emotionally significant garments, including: Wedding Dresses, Mother-of-the-Bride/Groom Dresses, Bridesmaids' Dresses, Prom Dresses, and all your Special Occasion Attire.

BRIDAL ALTERATIONS

Your wedding gown should fit as beautifully as the moment feels. We ensure every stitch, seam, and silhouette is perfected to highlight your elegance on your most unforgettable day.

Price Overview: Starts at $180 and goes up (price varies greatly by material and complexity).

Key Requirement: Must book 4-6 months in advance.

SPECIAL OCCASIONS

From galas to graduations, we tailor your attire to perfection so you can shine with confidence. Each adjustment is made with artistry and precision to complement your style and the spirit of your event.

Price Overview: Starts at $45 and goes up.

Key Requirement: Must book at least 2 weeks in advance (4 weeks during March - May).

GENTLEMEN'S HEMMING

A refined look begins with the right length and flawless tailoring. Our expert hemming ensures your trousers and suits drape cleanly and comfortably, giving you a sharp, confident appearance.

Price Overview: $20 for regular/ straight leg, $30 for wide leg.

Key Requirement: Must bring dress shoes or tennis shoes (to set the length) and belt/suspenders.

SPECIALTY SERVICES

CUSTOM GARMENTS

Crafting a garment from scratch. This is an artisanal service based on a client's specific design for those seeking a one-of-a-kind creation.

Every piece is an expression of individuality, cut and sewn to your exact measurements and preferences.

Pricing & Timeline: Requires a minimum of 3 months notice. Pricing is highly subjective to the client's budget and design complexity.

RUSH ORDER SERVICES

Alterations with a two week or less turnaround time. When time is limited, our skilled team delivers impeccable results without compromise. We prioritize your urgent needs, ensuring your garment is ready with precision, care, and punctuality.

Pricing & Timeline: Requires a Rush Fee starting at $25 (added to the alteration cost).

BODY MEASURING SERVICES

Taking precise body measurements for clients ordering clothes from outside vendors to minimize sizing mistakes and returns

Pricing & Timeline: 15$ flate fee.

Consultations & Pining Fee

Some alteration consultations for bridal gowns or special occasion dresses may require additional time due to the complexity of the garment or the number of questions about the alteration process. In such cases, a Pinning Fee will apply. This fee is non-refundable but will be credited toward the total cost of the alteration if the client proceeds with the service.

Cancellation Policy

Once a garment has been dropped off for alterations, it is added to the work schedule and may enter the cutting or fitting process. If the client decides to cancel after leaving the garment, a Cancellation Fee will be charged. The garment will be returned in its current condition at the time of cancellation, and no refunds will be issued for any work already started.

Disclaimer

Scheduling a consultation or appointment does not guarantee that we will be able to complete your project. RN Luxury Couture reserves the right to decline certain projects based on current workload, time frame, or design capacity.

Appointment Policy

Appointments are required, walk-ins are not accepted if we are not available.
